LA2050 is a community-guided initiative to drive and track progress toward a shared vision for the future of Los Angeles.

Our vision is that Los Angeles in 2050 will be the best place to learn, connect, play, create and the healthiest place to live.

Here’s how we’ll get there:

Goals

We worked with Angelenos to come up with the following five goals. All LA2050 activities work in support of these five goals.

Metrics

Metrics measure whether we are on track to reach our goals. They're backed by data, which helps us understand what's working and what's not.

For example

Per-capita crime rate is a metric that affects whether Los Angeles is becoming a safer place to play, and truancy rate measures whether LA is a great place to learn.

Reports

We compile the information we get from the goals and metrics into several reports which analyze the current state of human development and make informed projections about where we'll be in the year 2050. We also analyze projects submitted to the My LA2050 Grants Challenge to highlight useful trends and patterns from the frontlines of the community. We also analyze the impact of LA2050 as an initiative.
